Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Apple

Apple's website is a great example of visual hierarchy. The company uses a large hero image to draw the user's attention, followed by a clear call to action and a simple navigation menu.

Hick's Law

Amazon

Amazon's website is a great example of Hick's Law. The company uses a simple search bar as the main focus of the page, allowing users to quickly find what they are looking for.

White Space and Clean Design

Google

Google's website is a great example of white space and clean design. The company uses a simple color scheme and plenty of white space to create a clean and uncluttered look.
